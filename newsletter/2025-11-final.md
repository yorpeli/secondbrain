## Key Achievements

### Vendor Strategy & Optimization

We are overhauling our vendor approach, tightening how we select, work with, and continuously monitor vendors. Together with our partners and amazing cooperation with ops we identified and fixed a major flaw in non-ID document processing at one vendor that was sending ~17K documents/month into an unnecessary secondary flow, delaying approvals by up to four days and requiring extra offshore capacity. The fix not only fixes a flaw in one of the areas our customers complain about the most (time to approval) but it also reduces operational costs and is expected to lift conversion rate – which means a combined significant revenue impact. This is only the beginning of a change in our approach to vendors. We will keep the updates on these changes in the following newsletters.

**Vendor optimization capabilities for eKYC:** We have added the ability to determine in runtime the vendor of choice for eKYC/eKYB service so we can route traffic between different vendors and flows directly from an orchestration layer. This is an important first step in a way to a new vendor strategy.

**Why is this important:** This lets us test different vendors, flows on real traffic, and optimize our business impact based on real-time data and vendor performance. We can optimize for higher verification rates, better latency, and cost per check, while rolling out changes safely and gradually. This capability does not only allow us for real time optimization but also A/B test vendors.

### Unified PS Framework Enablement

We have established the foundational framework to support a unified set of requirements for PS, ensuring alignment with CLM compliance standards and providing a single source of truth.

**Why is it Important:** Solve the issue of fragmented compliance logic and ensure we comply with Citi bank AND CLM standards. External Stakeholders: Payment Services and their rollout of Unified Qualification Solution by December 15.

### POR Document Automation

Global legal entity POR review for directors and UBOs supported (rollout countries match individual/AR POR).

**Why is it Important:** Ensures document handling across different entities, allowing for increase first touch automatic approval estimated by 5%, reduce reopen by 10%, reduce # of documents submitted for LE POR to 1.5 and overall impact of 0.3% to the CLM conversion of companies.

Increased auto approval from 11.5% to about 14% by expanding country coverage and enhancing automation.

**Why is it Important:** Improving POR automation - being one of the most complex and important KYC document requirements – allows us to reduce reopen from 67% to 62% and approve more documents first touch.

### Migration Automation Tool

Process Management and Orchestration Capabilities: Version 1.0 of the tool is complete and operational. With elaborate flows and supporting migrations and cleanups. It has now moved to load testing for performance.

**Why is it Important:** This tool empowers operations, analysts, and product teams to efficiently manage transitions such as CLM migration and future licenses rollouts planned for 2026.

## Open Items

**Routing users from mobile to desktop** — CVR for CLM users using mobile devices is very low. The trend is clear especially for tier 1-2 companies with 3% approval rate on avg. on mobile app and 8% on mobile web in the last 2.5 months (vs. 32% on desktop). The direct unequivocal causes are unclear and therefore there are no quick solutions to the problem, we will, therefore, encourage users getting to mobile as an entry point to continue their onboarding flows on desktop.

**Transition of KYC management from InRule to P&Q** — Currently, both InRule and P&Q enforce KYC logic independently, leading to fragmented decision paths. The upcoming transition will strengthen compliance governance, simplify audits, reduces duplication of customer requirements, and improves operational accuracy.

**Increase onboarding CVR by 3% by replacing 'Showroom'** — Replace with access to a lean version of Payoneer account (without the ability to use the services). We intend to complete this by EOY. Why is this important? We believe that providing customers with the initial value of accessing the platform and exploring our offering will increase their motivation to complete onboarding.
