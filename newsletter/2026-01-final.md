To address gaps, January's launches including eCollection, document reduction, and real-time quality feedback, are specifically targeting areas in need of improvement, with early results expected in the February update.

## Key Achievements

### Localization

**eCollection for companies launched across 7 countries** — Using a single input (company registration number), the system pre-fills company details from local eVerification vendors. Live since late January for companies in Brazil, Colombia, Mexico, Turkey, Philippines, Argentina, and Pakistan. Customers provide one data point instead of four; when confirmed, two manual document requirements (company verification document and proof of company address) are removed. At full rollout: 9% KYC funnel completion uplift and 8% approval rate uplift expected.

**US document requirements reduced** — EIN requirement removed for eKYB-passing customers (electronic Know Your Business verification), live for 100% of US traffic (relevant for 78% of US companies). Proof of company address (POCA) removal when eVerification succeeds: live at 50% rollout. Targeting 7-day reduction in approval time.

**Country-specific results:**
- **Brazil:** Approval rate 41% → 47%. Time to approval down 25–31%.
- **UK:** Approval rate 41% → 47%. Time to approval improved 26% (5.8 → 4.3 days).
- Both markets saw reduced OPS document review volume from fewer required documents.

### Vendor Optimization

**Proof-of-residence (POR) address extraction** — Full address extraction via Persona launched January 13. Early data: Test group auto-approves approximately 1% more POR documents than control. Two additional countries opened (Turkey and Ukraine at 25% of CLM traffic). Next: expand to all global addresses.

**Document orchestration: Authenticity fallback** — When Resistant.AI returns an indecisive fraud check, the system now routes to Persona automatically (launched January 28). Targets the current 18% indecisive rate. In initial validation (5% of traffic), the test group approves approximately 4% more documents. At scale (approximately 45,000 documents monthly). This is being rolled out gradually together with OPS teams to ensure alignment with OPS workflows.

### KYC New Flow

**Real-time document quality feedback** — Launched a global A/B experiment: the system flags POR quality issues (blurriness, cropping, missing data) instantly during upload, replacing the days-long wait-then-reject cycle. We ran a first AB test that did not produce good results and we'll iterate to improve.

**CVD, POCA removal** — Rolled out to 7 countries. This is expected to generate 4% CLM CVR uplift in top tier countries. Remove 2 documents is a major friction reduction and a step forward to our seamless KYC approach.

**Global eCollection** — Rolled out to 7 countries. This is expected to generate 3% CLM CVR uplift in top tier countries. Retrieving company information directly from official data sources put us in the front of technology in terms of KYC process.

### Partners Rollout

**Partners Phase 2 live.** Rolled out January 19. Covers non-Lite Plan Tier 3, 30% of Tier 2, 10% of Tier 1, plus Banking and PWP (Pay With Payoneer) programs. 400+ programs, approximately 15,000 registrations/month expected. Approximately 1,000 registrations in the first two weeks — 5,000 required for statistically significant conversion data (mid-February target).
