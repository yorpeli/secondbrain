# PM Evolution — Manual / Augmented / AI-Native

**Status:** Synthesis, May 2026. Source for `decks/pm-evolution.pptx` (the artifact for Orrin / board).

## What this is

A two-slide companion to the AIR² jump deck, applied to the PM role specifically. The AIR² deck argues the team should jump from Manual past Augmented straight to AI-Native. This piece traces what that same arc looks like for the PM — what the role produces, where the time goes, how decisions get made, and what disappears at each phase.

It's the "what does this mean for me" companion to the AIR² argument. Built for Orrin (CPO), board-grade.

## The thesis

The PM role moves with the team along the same three-step arc: Manual → Augmented → AI-Native. The role doesn't reset — it sheds. **What the PM stops doing is as important as what the PM starts doing.**

The arc is observable, not vibes-based: where time goes, what artifacts are produced, what disappears, how decisions get made. Each row of the matrix is a measurable behavior, not an attitude.

## The matrix (Slide 1)

|  | A — Manual | B — Augmented | C — AI-Native |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Primary output** | PRDs, decks, backlog, status updates | Lighter specs, prototypes, AI-synthesized research | Executable specs, evaluator criteria, problem frames, ship → learn → improve cycles |
| **Where the time goes** | Coordination, writing artifacts, waiting on Data / design / engineering | Synthesis, prototyping, faster decisions | Product sense · judgment · action · customer proximity — ideation to production in days |
| **Decisions** | Defers to process; asks for permission; decisions held above the team | Makes the call with AI-aided context; still escalates by default | Owns intent; agents execute within blast radius; humans at go/no-go |
| **What disappears** | — | Waiting on Data, sequential handoffs, ten-page PRDs | Decks *about* decisions; "blocked on Data / research" as status; busy-as-productive |

The AI-Native "where the time goes" row — *Product Sense · Judgment · Action · Customer Proximity* — is lifted from Yonatan's internal "What Doesn't Get Outsourced" slide. These are the four pillars that survive every phase.

## What AI-Native unlocks (Slide 2)

Three things change at the team level when the PM operates AI-Native:

- **Velocity** — Ideation to production in days, not quarters. Co-solve replaces hand-off. The PM specifies intent upstream rather than approving drafts downstream. Cycle compression is structural, not cultural.
- **Autonomy** — Decisions live in the team. Blast-radius framework defaults L0 / L1 to the pod. Leadership asks for the decision and the reasoning — not the deck summarizing the research.
- **Bar** — A more senior PM. Business acumen, judgment, bias for action. AI fluency is table stakes, not a differentiator. Hiring, review, and what "good" means all move with the role. *The ceiling is getting higher, the floor as well.*

**Landing line:** *The AI-native PM: more senior, more autonomous, closer to the customer, measured by business outcomes.*
