Provenance: [deck: eBay V-team June 2nd final.pptx @ Shiri Cohen Hirsh's OneDrive /mine/Enterprise/Status/Vteam/]. Reconstructed 2026-06-10 from the deck's June 2 sections.

## Why the V-team Exists

- **Strategic importance:** eBay — 5-year client, Payoneer's largest Strategic Enterprise partner. $75M revenue / $6Bn volume directly, $90M+ ecosystem revenue, ~800 TICPs (~20% of Payoneer total).
- **Current challenge:** significant account-health pressure approaching a critical renewal (contract expires **Apr 2027**), primarily driven by sub-par KYC quality, stability, and measurability.
- **Response:** cross-functional V-team to align on moving eBay to **Green**, strengthen KYC quality transparently and measurably, hold a shared definition of success ahead of the next audit and renewal, and rebuild confidence in Payoneer's ability to support eBay at scale.
- **Outcome sought:** stabilized account health → better partnership optics → defended strategic position → positive renewal.

## Guiding Principles

1. **Own It Together** — functional leaders are joint owners of eBay's account health.
2. **Control the Optics** — communication is as critical as execution; strong rhythm and clear processes so eBay has visibility into tech/ops/service improvements.
3. **Focus and Elevate** — prioritize high-impact, realistic initiatives; proactively optimize rather than just react.
