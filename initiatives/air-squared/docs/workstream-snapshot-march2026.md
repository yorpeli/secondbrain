# AIR² — Workstream Snapshot | March 2026

**Mission:** Disruption in execution — ship AI-powered initiatives to production, fast. Move fast, might fail — value emerges from velocity.

---

## 1. End-State Vision — Target Architecture

**Full AI-native flow:** A fast-cycle AI lab that continuously discovers, builds, and validates high-value initiatives — surfacing quick wins from across Payoneer and accelerating ongoing AIR workstreams — then scaling successful MVPs to the relevant domain or retaining them under AIR².

### Building Blocks — Exist Today

- **Framework & methodology:** Discovery (≤3 days) → Build MVP (≤1.5 weeks) → Keep or Toss (after 3-week run) → Scale. 4-stage cycle defined and approved.
- **Leadership buy-in:** CPO-approved scope, team model, and operating framework. Two dedicated roles (PM + Engineer) approved for recruitment.
- **Team model:** AIR² operates as an AI-native pod (1 PM + 2 Engineers) — the first live instance of the AI-Native Team Operating Model. Co-Solve workflow: PM and engineers work simultaneously with AI, no handoffs, no sequential gates.
- **Team nucleus:** Lab lead in place. Operations manager assigned (Tom Tomer). Recruitment in progress for core pod.
- **Dual-track intake model:** Track 01 (expedite ongoing AIR initiatives) + Track 02 (BU OPEX initiatives)
- **Success metrics defined:** Throughput ("The Beat" — experiments to production/month) + Innovation Delta (estimated impact of scaled MVPs)

### Building Blocks — Missing

- **Core execution team [gating]:** 1 PM + 1 Engineer — roles approved, recruitment in progress via AI-native hiring flow (aligned with AI Team OS JDs shared with iTalent). Lab cannot run at full cadence without a complete pod.
- **Tooling & dev environment:** Secure, approved toolchain and sandbox not yet operational. Stack direction: flexible — whatever gets the job done within enterprise guardrails.
- **Prioritized initiative backlog:** Candidate initiatives identified but not yet scored and prioritized for first sprint.

### Main Workstream Outputs

- Experiments pushed to production per month (Throughput)
- MVPs validated and moved to Scale phase (Innovation Delta — cost savings or revenue impact)
- Reusable AI capabilities remaining under AIR² for further iteration

## 6. Risks & Trade-offs

### Key Risks

| Risk | Severity | Detail |
|------|----------|--------|
| **Team not staffed** | HIGH | Lab is pre-team — PM and Engineer roles approved but not yet filled. Everything downstream (backlog, Discovery, first MVP) is blocked until these are in place. |
| **Backlog not yet prioritized** | MEDIUM | Candidate ideas exist but haven't been scored. Risk of picking a low-signal first experiment that doesn't demonstrate the model's potential. |
| **Tooling environment not ready** | MEDIUM | Dev sandbox and approved toolchain not yet operational. Could block Build phase even after Discovery completes. |

### Key Trade-offs

**Speed vs. quality of first experiment:** The lab's credibility depends on a strong first MVP. Launching before the team is fully staffed risks picking the wrong experiment or executing below standard. The decision: wait for full team (slower start, higher-quality first cycle) vs. start Discovery with partial team (faster start, higher risk). Current approach: recruit first, then sprint.
