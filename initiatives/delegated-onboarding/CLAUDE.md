## What This Is

Delegated onboarding is Payoneer's model for enabling partners (resellers, platforms, marketplaces) to onboard their own sub-merchants through Payoneer's infrastructure. If cracked, this is potentially the biggest growth lever for the company — the ISP (Infrastructure Service Provider) vision. Meital Lahat Dekter owns this domain, ramping up from ops mastery toward strategic positioning.
