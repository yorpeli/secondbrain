# Yonatan's response to Yaron's CLM Q3 Investment Plan (2026-06)

> Top-down strategy layer. Predates and outranks the bottoms-up team workbooks. Pair with `CLM_Q3_Investment_Plan-Yaron.docx`.

## Three priorities
1. India License.
2. AI Modernization - all modernization efforts should feed into business impact and AI-focused outcomes.
3. FFT Improvements.

## Strategic Frame: Licensing as an AI Moat
Regulatory licenses across jurisdictions are a barrier we should keep high - one of our competitive moats. AI-driven KYX automation built on top of a licensed, compliant base compounds into a defensible advantage. This reframes India, periodic reviews, and the broader Licenses track as moat-building base-layer investment.

## Standalone items (NOT to be folded into existing objectives)
1. **Claude KYC Agent** - complete KYC agent incl. AI document understanding + field extraction. The flagship AI application. Outcome: higher approval CVR + reduced manual touch -> higher FFT. Metric: extraction accuracy + **60% auto-resolution on D-leads**.
2. **Vendor orchestration & QA layers** - complete real-time vendor orchestration logic + redundancy per KYX step, + vendor QA framework + agentic-KYC QA benchmark. Metric: global match-rate + KYX completion rate.
3. **Cost-to-serve / auto-decisioning** - reduce manual-review load via auto-approval + policy automation. Metric: manual-review reduction + cost-per-onboarding.
4. **Top of funnel, Lead Scoring & mobile acquisition** - lead quality/scoring + signup-to-submission conversion + dedicated mobile-first & desktop UX track. We currently start at registration; this addresses the front half of the funnel.
5. **KYC New Flow** - the requirements-submission + eCollection engine beneath FDC and Approval->FFT. Currently at-risk, crowded out by rollout; needs additional work.
6. **Compliance - periodic reviews & cleanup** - regulatory periodic-review obligations (PEL + UK SDD; Australia + Canada) + CLM migration. Deadline-driven, mandatory.
7. **Monitoring and Alerting** - elevate from an open dependency to a resourced foundation track for ongoing real-time health/performance tracking.
8. **Ongoing lifecycle** - the management half of CLM: re-verification, reactivation, expansion beyond add-company.
9. **China. TICP.**
10. **Rollout stabilization & sequencing** - post-rollout stability + defect remediation is the principal constraint on Q3 capacity; commitments should be sequenced against this drain, not layered on top.
